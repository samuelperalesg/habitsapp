Healthy Habits to Focus On:

  • Eating Healthy - This helps us maintain a healthy mood and body!
  • Sleeping Early - Getting to bed on time ensures that we have enough energy for tomorrow!
  • Brushing Your Teeth - Having healthy teeth ensures that our teeth will not rot when we are older :)

Unhealthy Habits to Be Aware Of:

  • Snacking (High Sugar or High Salt Content) - This may create an imbalance in our degestive process which can affect mood & energy if we are not careful.
  • Sleeping Late - Not having a sleeping schedule can make our days very unpredictable, we should minimize how long we stay up till.
  • Watching TV - Too much entertainment can lead us astray of our goals, even if our shows are so good, we do not have to bingle the whole thing in one sitting.
